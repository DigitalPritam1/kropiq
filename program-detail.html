Programme overview

Most farm decisions get made on instinct, habit or what worked for a neighbour last season. This programme replaces that with a system. You learn to read your own numbers — cost per acre, break-even yield, working capital cycle — and to decide which crop, which input and which market on evidence.

You apply every framework to your own land as you go. By the end you hold a costed crop plan, a working farm P&L and a pricing approach for your produce — reviewed by a mentor who farms.
