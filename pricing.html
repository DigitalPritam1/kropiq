Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings whenever you choose. Your access continues until the end of the billing cycle you have already paid for.

Do you work with FPOs and agricultural colleges?

Yes. We run cohort programmes with farmer producer organisations, co-operatives and agricultural colleges, priced per cohort. Talk to our team to plan one for your members.

How do I pay?

We accept UPI, net banking, debit and credit cards. Cohort partners can pay by bank transfer against an invoice.
