Why Kropiq

Better decisions build better farms

We do not teach farming techniques alone. We teach the decisions that determine whether a season turns a profit.

Decisions, not just techniques

Every module ends in a decision framework — which crop, which input, which market, at which price. You leave knowing how to choose, not just how to do.

Costed to the acre

We work in cost per acre, break-even yield and return on every input. You build a farm P&L you actually use through the season.

Proof of capability

Mentors who farm

Field-tested, not theoretical

Built for your district

Kropiq MentorIrrigation That Pays For Itself · Module four

My borewell bill is climbing every month. Do I water longer or more often?

On your sandy loam, increase frequency, not duration. Long sets push water below the root zone — you pay for the pumping and the crop never sees it. Three 12-minute cycles, four hours apart, holds moisture where the roots are — roughly ₹2,100 a month off your power bill. Shall we cost it out?
